# Persona: "Job-Seeking Linh"

## Descriptive
*   **Narrative:** Linh is a 22-year-old final-year IT student in Ho Chi Minh City who has completed several class projects in React, Node.js, and MySQL. Linh has started applying for internships and junior roles on VietnamWorks, TopCV, and LinkedIn, but receives few interview invitations and is not sure whether the problem is the CV, the role fit, or missing skills.
*   **Demographic:** 22 years old, based in Ho Chi Minh City, final-year university student, part-time intern or freelance project contributor, middle-income family background.
*   **Psychographic:** Practical, ambitious, and digitally fluent. Values honesty and career progress. Willing to learn aggressively, but wants a clear path and does not want to fake skills or overclaim experience.

## Motivations
*   Wants to land a first meaningful tech role within the next 3 to 6 months.
*   Wants to understand which roles are realistic today instead of wasting time applying blindly.
*   Wants a CV that is ATS-friendly and tailored to a specific job without lying.
*   Wants a concrete roadmap showing what to learn next to become a stronger match for better roles.

## Interactions
*   Uses a laptop as the primary device for job applications, coding practice, and CV editing.
*   Checks job boards and LinkedIn frequently during internship and graduation seasons.
*   Uses GitHub, YouTube, online courses, and AI chat tools to learn technical skills.
*   Distrusts black-box tools that rewrite a CV automatically without review or explanation.

## Pain Points
*   Does not know whether the current CV is ATS-friendly.
*   Struggles to decode vague or inflated job description language.
*   Uses one generic CV for many applications, which leads to weak fit.
*   Does not know which missing skills are critical for one specific JD versus just nice to have.
*   Fears that AI tools will invent experience or unsupported skills.
*   Learns from random courses without knowing whether they improve employability.

## Moments that Matter
*   The first time Linh sees a promising JD and wonders, "Am I actually qualified for this?"
*   The moment Linh uploads a CV and needs to trust the ATS conversion before using it.
*   The decision to click "Generate CV for This Job" for one selected JD.
*   Receiving a rejection or no response after multiple applications.
*   Finishing a roadmap item and wanting to reflect that progress in a truthful new CV version.

# Journey Map: AI Career Path Copilot

## Discover
Linh applies to several internships and junior tech jobs using the same CV and gets little or no response. After seeing advice from classmates, job-seeking communities, or a university career center, Linh becomes aware that the issue may not just be skill level, but also ATS formatting, CV-to-JD fit, and lack of clarity about which jobs are realistic.

## Aware
Linh discovers AI Career Path Copilot and sees a clear promise: upload one CV, convert it into an ATS-friendly master version, review it manually, and then use it to find suitable jobs. The product stands out because it does not auto-apply, does not auto-claim fake skills, and does not rewrite the CV without user review.

## Convert
Linh uploads a CV. The system generates a structured ATS-friendly CV draft. Linh reviews the conversion, edits any mistakes, and approves it. The system then searches job platforms and returns a ranked list of suitable JDs. Linh selects one promising JD and manually clicks "Generate CV for This Job." The system generates one tailored CV draft based only on the approved ATS CV and supported evidence. Linh reviews that output before deciding to use it.

## Retain (Retent)
When Linh sees that a selected JD is only a partial match, the system generates one roadmap for that job. Linh returns weekly to complete roadmap items, attach proof such as a GitHub project or certificate, and then click to generate an updated JD-specific CV version. The product becomes part of an ongoing application workflow rather than a one-time CV editor.

## Advocate
After getting better-fit applications, interview callbacks, or clearer learning direction, Linh shares the tool with classmates and junior developer friends. The strongest referral moment is when Linh can say, "It showed me which jobs were realistic, helped me clean up my CV for ATS, and told me exactly what I was missing for one specific role."

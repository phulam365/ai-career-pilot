# Pitch Deck Outline: AI Career Path Copilot

## Presenting
**Presenter:** Product Lead for AI Career Path Copilot.  
**The Hook:** "Thousands of early-career tech candidates in Vietnam are applying to jobs with one generic CV and getting rejected without knowing whether the problem is ATS format, role fit, or missing skills. They are not lacking effort. They are lacking signal."

## Problem Statement
Final-year students, fresh graduates, and junior tech workers in Vietnam face a fragmented transition from learning to employment. Job boards show openings but not truthful readiness. Learning platforms show courses but not which gaps block one specific job. Candidates waste time sending generic CVs, guessing which roles fit, and taking random courses without knowing whether those efforts improve employability.

## Solutions
**AI Career Path Copilot** starts from one uploaded CV. It converts that CV into an ATS-friendly master version with required user review, finds suitable jobs from supported job platforms, and lets the user manually generate one tailored CV for one selected JD. If the CV is not yet a full fit, the product generates one roadmap for that exact JD and later supports evidence-based CV updates after the user learns more.

## Desirability
Users want faster clarity, not more generic advice. The product addresses immediate pain:
*   "Which jobs can I realistically apply for now?"
*   "Is my CV ATS-friendly?"
*   "How do I tailor my CV without lying?"
*   "What should I learn next for this exact job?"

The human-in-the-loop flow is a key differentiator. Users review the ATS conversion, manually trigger JD-specific CV generation, and review outputs before using them.

## Viability
The strongest business path is B2B2C:
*   Universities and bootcamps want better graduate employability outcomes.
*   Training providers want clearer skill-gap journeys tied to real jobs.
*   Hiring partners benefit from better-aligned early-career candidates.

A premium B2C layer can support exports, advanced roadmap tracking, interview prep, and richer matching insights, but institutional distribution is the stronger initial wedge.

## Feasibility
This product is technically realistic as an MVP:
*   CV parsing and ATS restructuring are feasible with review safeguards.
*   Semantic matching can rank CV-to-JD fit.
*   JD-specific CV generation can be controlled through evidence-based rules.
*   Roadmaps can be scoped to a limited number of early-career tech roles first.

The main execution dependency is reliable access to job descriptions through approved platform integrations, APIs, or licensed job feeds.

# Product Roadmap: AI Career Path Copilot

## Vision
Every early-career tech candidate in Vietnam can move from a raw CV to a clear, truthful, and job-specific action plan with confidence.

## Strategy
We will win by starting from the user's existing CV, not from generic career advice. The product will combine ATS normalization, CV-to-JD matching, controlled JD-specific CV generation, and job-specific learning roadmaps in one workflow. Trust is the differentiator: no auto-apply, no auto-generation without a button click, and no unsupported skill claims.

## Capabilities
*   **Theme 1: ATS Master CV Conversion and Review**
    Convert uploaded CVs into a structured ATS-friendly master version and require user review before approval.
*   **Theme 2: CV-to-JD Matching and Explanation**
    Search job platforms, rank relevant JDs, and explain why a job is a strong, partial, or weak match.
*   **Theme 3: JD-Specific CV Generation**
    Let the user manually generate one tailored CV for one selected JD and review the output before using it.
*   **Theme 4: JD-Specific Gap Roadmaps**
    Generate one roadmap for one selected JD when the user's CV does not fully match the role.
*   **Theme 5: Evidence-Based CV Updates**
    Allow users to update a JD-specific CV version only from completed and supported roadmap items.

## Success Metrics
*   **Happiness:** User trust score for ATS conversion and JD-specific CV generation.
*   **Adoption:** Percentage of uploaded CVs that are successfully reviewed and approved as ATS master CVs.
*   **Engagement:** Percentage of users who open matched JDs and manually click "Generate CV for This Job."
*   **Task Success:** Percentage of users who export or save at least one reviewed JD-specific CV.
*   **Retention:** Weekly return rate for users with active job-specific roadmaps.
*   **Outcome Metric:** Interview callback rate or application progression rate for users who use JD-specific CVs.

## Time Horizon
*   **Q1 (Now):** CV upload, ATS master CV conversion, required review flow, and matched job ranking.
*   **Q2 (Next):** JD-specific CV generation with manual trigger, output review, and export.
*   **Q3 (Later):** JD-specific roadmap generation, roadmap progress tracking, and evidence-based CV updates.
*   **Q4 (Later):** Partner job feeds, university and bootcamp dashboards, and outcome analytics.
